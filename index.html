Hi there! It's Sia this side! 🌻

First of all, thank you so much for dropping in to know more about me! ✨

So yes, first things first — I'm a final year student at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology, Noida, pursuing my Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering.

Getting to the real me: I'm a pretty curious person interested in almost everything. I once read a quote — "Nearly everything is interesting if you go into it deeply enough" — and we can pretty much say I live by it.

Right since I've been a little girl, I've always been trying new things — Kathak, poetry, space science, historical research, karate... I've dipped my hands in a whole lot of things.

Even now I still am that little girl, just exploring entirely different domains. In tech, I really enjoy going deep into the tiny things that power mega systems — so system design and Computer Organization & Architecture form one of my core interests.

Having been a philomath all my life, numbers intrigue me. I've been working with Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and AI lately. It's really fun to see how the most complex concepts always ultimately break down to math. ✨

As for coding — that's what really fuels me. Always found tinkering with a piece of code, experimentation really gets going there! Currently building across domains, exploring how foundational concepts scale into real systems.

And yes — I'm a storyteller too. Whether through words or code, I always try to communicate as authentically as possible. My love for writing powers my blog — A Readaholics Panorama 🌸 — a space where I fuse my multiple sides and craft narratives that make my life.

An ardent music lover, I can easily be found humming my latest favourite, my laptop keys clicking and a writing pad for sure lying by my side!

— Sia 🌻
